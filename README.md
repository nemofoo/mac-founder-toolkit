# Mac Founder Toolkit

A curated list of Mac tools for busy founders. Recording demos, editing content, writing docs, and shipping product — all from macOS.

## Screen Recording

| Tool | Description | Pricing |
|------|-------------|---------|
| [ShipClip](https://shipclip.app) | Native Mac screen recorder with zoom-to-click, multi-track audio, camera PiP with background removal, and shareable links with viewer analytics. | Paid |

## Video & Audio Production

| Tool | Description | Pricing |
|------|-------------|---------|
| [Final Cut Pro](https://www.apple.com/final-cut-pro/) | Apple's professional video editing app. Magnetic timeline, HDR, and Metal-accelerated rendering. | Paid |
| [Logic Pro](https://www.apple.com/logic-pro/) | Professional music production and audio editing. MIDI, spatial audio, and a massive sound library. | Paid |

## Writing & Notes

| Tool | Description | Pricing |
|------|-------------|---------|
| [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md) | Markdown-based knowledge base with local-first storage and plugin ecosystem. | Free / Paid |
| [Tota](https://www.heytota.com) | On-device text to speech. Listen to articles, docs, and notes naturally. | Paid |

## Productivity

| Tool | Description | Pricing |
|------|-------------|---------|
| [ebb](https://ebb.cool) | Focus timer that fades your screen to help you stay in flow. | Free / Paid |
| [GroupCtrl](https://github.com/brodmo/GroupCtrl) | Hotkey management — group and toggle keyboard shortcuts across apps. | Free |
